Comprehensive guide for planning and running successful hackathons, from first-timers to experienced organizers. This skill covers timelines, sponsors, judging, logistics, prizes, and post-event follow-up.

## When to Use

- Planning, organizing, or running a hackathon
- Questions about hackathon logistics, timelines, or budgets
- Sponsor outreach or management advice
- Setting up judging formats, criteria, or prize strategies

## Activation Keywords

The skill activates when your prompt includes terms like:

`organize a hackathon`, `plan a hackathon`, `hackathon organizing tips`, `how to run a hackathon`, `hackathon logistics`, `hackathon sponsor`, `hackathon judging setup`

## What's Included

The skill bundles two reference files that the AI agent uses for context:

- **planning-and-logistics.md** — Timeline, venue, food, day-of operations, and post-event checklist
- **judging-and-prizes.md** — Judging formats, criteria design, judge recruitment, prize strategy

## Topics Covered

- **Planning timeline** — What to do 3-6 months, 1-3 months, and 1-4 weeks before the event
- **Venue and logistics** — WiFi, power, breakout rooms, presentation areas
- **Sponsorships** — Tiered packages, outreach strategy, sponsor management
- **Registration** — Managing signups, dietary restrictions, t-shirt sizes
- **Judging** — Formats (expo-style vs. panel), criteria design, judge recruitment, scoring
- **Prizes** — Prize strategy, categories, sponsor prizes
- **Day-of operations** — Schedule, volunteer coordination, communication channels
- **Post-event** — Follow-up emails, feedback surveys, content sharing

## Example Prompts

- "How do I organize my first hackathon?"
- "What's a good timeline for planning a 200-person hackathon?"
- "How should I structure my sponsorship packages?"
- "What's the best judging format for 50+ submissions?"
