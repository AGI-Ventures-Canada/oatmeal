Everything you need to know to prepare for, compete in, and get the most out of a hackathon. This skill covers preparation, ideation, building, presenting, and networking.

## When to Use

- Preparing for an upcoming hackathon as a participant
- Looking for hackathon tips, strategies, or advice
- First hackathon and want to know what to expect
- Want to improve your demo or presentation
- Looking for project ideas or team formation strategies

## Activation Keywords

The skill activates when your prompt includes terms like:

`attending a hackathon`, `hackathon tips`, `how to win a hackathon`, `first hackathon`, `hackathon preparation`, `hackathon project ideas`, `hackathon demo tips`

## What's Included

The skill bundles two reference files that the AI agent uses for context:

- **building-and-strategy.md** — Technical strategy, MVP approach, tech stack choices, time management
- **presenting-and-networking.md** — Demo tips, what judges look for, networking, and post-hackathon follow-up

## Topics Covered

- **What to bring** — Laptop, charger, power bank, headphones, snacks, and more
- **Pre-event preparation** — Dev environment setup, starter templates, sponsor API research
- **Team formation** — Finding teammates, complementary skills, roles
- **Ideation** — Brainstorming techniques, evaluating ideas, scope management
- **Building** — MVP-first approach, tech stack choices, time management
- **Presenting** — Demo structure, storytelling, what judges look for
- **Networking** — Making connections, following up after the event

## Example Prompts

- "This is my first hackathon, what should I expect?"
- "How do I prepare for a hackathon next weekend?"
- "What do judges look for in hackathon demos?"
- "How should I manage my time during a 24-hour hackathon?"
